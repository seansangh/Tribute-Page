Timeline of Albert Einstein's life:

  • 1879 - Albert Einstein was born in Ulm, Germany.
  • 1881 - Albert Einstein's family moves to Munich, Germany.
  • 1894 - Albert Einstein's family moves from Munich, Germany to Pavia, Italy.
  • 1895 - Albert is sent to Aarau, Switzerland to finish high school.
  • 1896 - Albert finishes high school and goes to a university (ETH) in Zurich, Switzerland.
  • 1900 - Albert graduates from ETH with a physics degree.
  • 1903 - Albert marries Mileva Maric, a Hungarian classmate of his from ETH.
  • 1904 - Mileva gives birth to Hans Albert Einstein in Berne, Switerzland.
  • 1905 - Einstein's Theory of Relativity takes off. Einstein formulates his famous equation e=mc2. Winstein publishes four papers.
  • 1909 - Albert resigns from the Patent Office and is hired as a theoretical physics professor at Zurich University.
  • 1910 - Albert's son, Eduard Einstein, is born in Munich, Germany.
  • 1914 - Einstein separates from wife Mileva.
  • 1915 - Albert completes his general Theory of Relativity.
  • 1919 - Albert marries his cousin Elsa.
  • 1921 - Albert is awarded the Nobel Prize in physics.
  • 1933 - Albert leaves Germany and emigrates to the United States of America.
  • 1940 - Albert becomes a US citizen.
  • 1955 - Albert died on April 18th at the age of 76.
